Our Data Policy (written so people can read it) When we use/collect data: Location data is referenced on your phone when calculating the tipping amount Data is only ever sent to us if you tap the green submit (✔) button Types of data we collect: Location - We collect your “tipping region” so we can better learn how people tip in your area. The total of your bill - so we can see if people generally tip differently on different sized bills. The amount that you tipped - so we know how much you tipped… duhhh. How we use your data: Data is always saved unidentifiable, bundled together in a database Once data has been sent to us there is no way (or reason) to trace it back to you We run algorithms over the data we collect to try to better understand tipping practices across the world, making our app smarter and better for our users!
